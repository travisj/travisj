Ryan Adams in HD

Oct 19, 2007

Yesterday Cam told me that Ryan Adams and the Cardinals were playing a live concert on MHD. I quickly set the DVR and got home from work today with the Yahoo! Music/Nissan Live Music Set ready to go. It was only half an hour, but it was awesome. Seeing Ryan Adams play in HD is the only way to go. If you have MHD, you definitely need to check this set out (it’s on all the time).

In other Ryan Adams news, he’s got another album coming out next week (believe it or not!).
