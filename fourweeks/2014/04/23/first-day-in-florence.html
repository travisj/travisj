First day in Florence

Apr 23, 2014

Yesterday we left Rome and arrived in Florence. To maximize our time in Florence, we decided to get the Firenze Card, which gives us access to most museums and lets us wait in the priority lines, which usually move very fast. 

We made quick use of the card by first climbing to the top of the Duomo and were rewarded with amazing views in all directions. Once we got to the top, we got to spend as much time as we wanted looking out over Florence. Coming down was fun as we had to negotiate the small staircases and free space with people coming up.

After the Duomo we set out to find dinner and followed dinner up with some gelato recommended to us by a friend.

We had a great first day in Florence.
