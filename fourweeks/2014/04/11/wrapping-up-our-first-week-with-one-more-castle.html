Wrapping up our first week with one more castle

Apr 11, 2014

We began the day in Füssen with a plan to see Neuschwanstein Castle and then catch the trains to Vienna. We are on the last train now (wifi on the Austrian Railjet!) and so far things have exceeded all my expectations.

Neuschwanstein Castle was awesome. The views of the castles, the mountains and the valleys were all spectacular. 

We'll be in Vienna for almost three full days. We don't have too many plans, but I'd like to go to The Prater and ride a trolley around the ring of central Vienna.

If you have any Vienna recommendations, please comment. Thanks!
