Vienna

Apr 12, 2014

We packed a lot into today - in a good way. We started out the day with a short run and ended at the Naschmarkt for a few baked goods.

We then headed over to the marathon expo to pick up my number. Nothing too exciting happened at the expo.

The expo center was right by the Prater so we, of course, headed there after. We explored the grounds a bit and then rode the Wiener Riesenrad (German for "Viennese giant wheel"). We got some great views of the city and beyond - it was such a clear day.

Next we took off for Schönbrunn Palace, built by the Habsburg family. We went on the audio tour and saw 40 impressive rooms. It was a bit hard to keep all the different kings, queens and their children straight, but it was a good tour. The grounds were even more impressive, but we didn't walk around them too much. I am hoping to find some time before we leave to spend some more time in the gardens.

We don't have too many plans for tomorrow, other than the marathon, but I hope to see something after - even if it's just back up the street to the Naschmarkt for more baked goods.

Danke für das Lesen.
