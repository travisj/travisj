Saturday run-around

Jun 14, 2014

I went for a run this morning and since it was a beautiful day, I decided to take a few pictures. 

Starting off the run on our quiet street. al di la has already cleaned the sidewalks, but nothing else is happening.

I ran across to Columbia st. to catch the bike bath and make my way up toward Brooklyn Bridge Park.

I think this is Pier 5, which has lots of soccer fields.

I took the new path and headed toward the brand new Pier 2.

Pier 2, the new fitness pier.

Got back to the main path and had my first good view of the bridge.

I made it down to Pier 1 and took a quick stop for some photos of Manhattan and bridges.

I then headed under the Brooklyn Bridge and over to the carousel.

That's obviously me in the reflection.

Looks like there was going to be a viewing party here later, my guess is the World Cup.

Just a street scene from Dumbo. We don't come over here often, but it looks like somewhere we should return to.

I made my way back to the entrance of the Manhattan Bridge.

Climbing the bridge.

My favorite view in the entire city.

I don't know what this says or means, but every time I see it, I wonder about it.

This cop tried to hit me as he rolled through a stop sign. I stared him down, kept running, then snapped this as he passed. I made sure to wait for the walk sign before running through the next light, though.

Making my way back to the Brooklyn Bridge.

Look at all those people. I guess I didn't make it to the bridge early enough. Lots of dodging people and bikes for the mile and a half.

Something happened to my phone's camera and the next few pictures came out of focus. I probably enabled some setting without knowing.

"All is fare in love and Brooklyn"

Smith St. and Atlantic.

Made it back to 4th Ave.

And done!

Wrapping it up with a couple of bagel sandwiches with these two.
